# TalkAR Beta Testing Guide

## Welcome Beta Testers!

Thank you for participating in the TalkAR Beta program. Your feedback is crucial to making TalkAR the best AR experience possible.

## Installation

1. You will receive an email invitation to join the beta testing program
2. Click the link in the email to opt-in to the beta
3. Download and install the TalkAR Beta app from the Play Store
4. The app icon will show "TalkAR Beta" to distinguish it from the production version

## Testing Tasks

Please complete the following tasks and provide feedback after each:

### Task 1: Basic AR Scanning
- [ ] Open the TalkAR Beta app
- [ ] Point your camera at 3 different product posters
- [ ] Verify that the AR avatar appears for each poster
- [ ] Rate your experience (1-5 stars)

### Task 2: Language Selection
- [ ] Scan a poster
- [ ] Change the language setting
- [ ] Rescan the same poster
- [ ] Verify the avatar speaks in the new language
- [ ] Rate your experience

### Task 3: Voice Interaction
- [ ] Scan a poster and wait for the avatar to appear
- [ ] Try asking the avatar a question using voice
- [ ] Verify you receive a response
- [ ] Rate your experience

### Task 4: App Stability
- [ ] Close the app mid-session
- [ ] Reopen the app
- [ ] Scan a poster again
- [ ] Verify everything works correctly
- [ ] Rate your experience

## What to Look For

### Performance
- How long does it take for the AR avatar to appear?
- Is the video playback smooth?
- Does the app feel responsive?

### User Experience
- Is the interface intuitive?
- Are buttons easy to find and use?
- Is the feedback modal clear and easy to use?

### Issues to Report
- App crashes
- AR detection failures
- Slow loading times
- Confusing UI elements
- Any other problems you encounter

## Providing Feedback

After each AR session, you'll see a feedback modal asking you to:
1. Rate your experience (1-5 stars)
2. Optionally add comments about what worked well or what didn't

Please be as detailed as possible in your comments!
